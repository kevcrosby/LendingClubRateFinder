Correlation: Interest Rate vs ...

In order to calculate optimal interest rate for a lender/requestor, different input variables were analyzed to determine the overall effect on interest rate. As the graphs show, investment amount and annual income have minimal correlation to interest rate, while dept to income has a relatively strong correlation. Credit grade, which is a amalgamation of the three aforementioned variables, has the strongest correlation to interest rate.

What Do You Need Money For?

While Debt Consolidation accounted for nearly 59% of all investments, people looking for cash flow for their small businesses received the largest average investment. Perhaps peers offering loans were feeling a bit like Shark Tank and were ready to invest!

It was the Heat of the Intrest

The above heat maps plot interest rate and investment amounts across the United States. On a state by state basis, the interest rate, on average, is inversely proportional to the amount loaned. That is to say, the states that received more loans have generally lower interest rates.
